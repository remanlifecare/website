    Anti-infective

    Anti-infective medicines cure or prevent infections. It is when the immune system refuses to kill pathogenic microorganisms within the body that infectious diseases occur.Anti-infective therapy is usually soughtwhen oral medications cannot be the solution to such infections. Antibiotics, antifungals, and antivirals are administered intravenously, as part of anti-infective therapy.

    Reman offers anti-infective therapies for a number of infectious diseases like abscesses, bacterial endocarditis, cellulitis, chronic urinary tract infections, community acquired pneumonia, osteomyelitis, pancreatitis, post-transplant, sepsis and wound infections. Our therapy division is expanding to cater to more conditions.

    Gastro

    Reman’s gastro therapy wing is envisaged to address conditions like gastritis, gastroenteritis and gastric ulcer.Inflation or erosion of the lining of the stomach, which can be acute, is gastritis. Gastroenteritis is tricky. The definition may limit the condition to irregularities in the stomach and intestines, but at times, the whole of the body might have to bear the consequences.Basically, it is the swelling and inflation of the intestines. The typical symptoms are diarrhoea and vomiting accompanied by stomach pain, cramping, fever, nausea, and sometimes headache. Gastro conditions can leave you dehydrated. Dry skin, dry mouth, feeling thirsty and lightheaded; it can turn the whole world upside down. Peptic ulcer is yet another gastro affliction.

    Dental Care

    Reman’s therapy under this segment proposes clinical and therapeutic care. Dental problems can be myriad: cavities, bleeding or swollen gums, toothache, chronic bad breath, loose teeth, receding gums, cracked or broken teeth and frequent dry mouth, to name a few. Causes can be as diverse as the conditions. Smoking, poor brushing habits, frequent snacking on sugary foods and beverages, diabetes, genetics, infections, hormonal changes, acid reflux etc. can be culprits in dental problems.

    It has been evidenced in the light of recent studies that there is a solid connection between declining oral health and underlying systemic conditions. In the wake of this, Reman’s Dental care therapy wing collaborates with diverse pharma bigwigs to address existing and evolving dental conditions.
