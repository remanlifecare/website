Who we are?

Altruism and entrepreneurship go hand in hand, at Reman Lifecare. Incorporated in 2017, Reman has showcased steady growth backed by its commitment to innovation, service and quality.

We have been doing our bit to make the world equipped to fight diseases, ever since our humble beginnings. Our business stretches pan India, and we are working on innovative products for various markets across India. Our current growth largely owes to our unwavering passion to provide care for the invalid, we believe.

To ensure that our services meet the expected standard and quality, Reman employs sophisticated infrastructure and futuristic technologies. Our marketing services are always solution based so that our clients can confidently draw on them en route to their solid progress. What makes us standout of the crowd is our potential for providing pharma marketing solutions that are economical and sustainable.

Milestones

It is in Reman’s innate design to contribute to functionally confronting global health challenges. A staunch pool of ingenious researchers get Reman running dynamically. We have made our presence known in the market through our professionalism and our ardour to set foot in new ventures.

2020

Reman announced India’s First TOOTHPASTE infused with VITAMIN B12.

2019

Foemina Lifecare was announced with the goal to serve the wellbeing of the new age woman.

2018

Reman announced India’s first ANTI-POLLUTION Sunscreen.

Reman’s journey towards being one of the most sought-after pharma firms in India was never an easy one. We are what we are on account of our key players’ relentless efforts and steadfast dedication.

Meet Our Team

Ahmer Rizvi

THE CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER
Ahmer Rizvi

Ahmer Rizvi is an entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is the founder and CEO of Reman. Being the highest-ranking executive at Reman, Ahmer’s principal responsibilities include making crucial corporate decisions, superintending the general procedures and assets of Reman as well as being the public face of the company. Ahmer supervises operations of Reman, carries out plans, manages human as well as financial resources. His leadership has played a key role in aiding Reman bag the recognition of a home grown pharmaceutical which has plenty potential to go global. His expertise that spans over a decade in the healthcare industry has helped Reman align its fundamental efficacies in line with its salient goals.

Ahmer's skills in strategic planning, product development, inventory management, marketing and business development have turned out to be crucial in transforming Reman into one of the leading pharma marketing companies in India. His vision has greatly contributed to shaping the current work culture and corporate structure of Reman. In his capacity as the CEO of Reman, Ahmer intervenes not only in matters of prominent tactical decisions that determine the company’s overall growth, but makes himself available to oversee the everyday functions as well. Being the motivator and entrepreneur that he is, Ahmer ardently champions the cause of employee motivation and work-life balance in Reman. He effectively manages the human resources of Reman. He devices company policies, and formulates courses of action in operations, marketing, delivery and quality of services that the company provides. His expertise in sales management and entrepreneurship facilitates Ahmer to manage Reman’s resources within the budget regulations, prudently. He ensures, from time to time that Reman, its vision and services are invariably presented in a strong, affirmative manner to relevant stakeholders.

Having completed his B.Tech in Petroleum Engineering from IIT, Dhanbad, Ahmer started off his career as an Executive at CFPL Healthcare. Over the course of 6 years, Ahmer’s career manifested a steady growth from that of a humble Executive to the Assistant Director of CFPL Healthcare. Backed by the mastery gained through experience, Ahmer co-founded Reman in 2017. He chairs the Executive Committee at Reman. Ahmer currently serves as Executive Director to Maxel Leadership Council, as well.

Arshad Rizvi

THE CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER
Arshad Rizvi

Arshad assumed office as Reman’s Chief Operation Officer, in 2018. He mainly focuses on executing the company’s business plan, according to Reman's business model. Arshad is tasked with managing issues including marketing, development, sales and delivery. As COO, Arshad stipulates comprehensive goals for Reman’s performance and growth, and identifies every so often, what the company lacks in fulfilling the goals and resolves them. He collaborates with the CEO in planning and executing Reman’s organizational vision and hiring requirements, ensuring that the employees are committed to the cause of carrying out the vision of Reman. Arshad is entrusted with analyzing the effectiveness of Reman's business strategies and finding ways to improve them to take the operations to next level. He ensures that performance expectations are met by motivating Reman’s employees to deliver their finest performance and commitment; thus overseeing performance management, employee productivity and employee retention. Arshad manages the company’s relationship with clients and vendors, too. He undertakes transfiguring the company strategy into actionable initiatives.

His role in ensuring the transition of Reman in terms of commercialization has been pivotal. The company invests its hope for substantial success in Arshad’s headship. Standard operation and regulation of the pharmaceutical fall under the purview of Arshad’s core responsibilities. He oversees the day-to-day administrative functions, the work of the executives, internal affairs and the supply chain as well. His expertise in marketing, development, management affairs, quality control, production and client relations has been a true game changer as far as Reman’s evolution is concerned. Being the COO of Reman, Arshad plans operations schemes, presents company strategies to employees, and helps human resources expand the fundamental teams that are crucial to the functioning of the company. He professionally organizes all the activities and operations at Reman to ensure that business strategy is properly supported and is in alignment with the clients’ requirements.

Arshad did his undergrad in Computer Science Engineering at the prestigious B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai. He began his career as a software developer at Cognizant Technology Solutions.

Dr. M. Ashraf Rizvi

ADVISOR
Dr. M. Ashraf Rizvi

Reman has at its helm Dr. M. Ashraf Rizvi for Advisor. Dr. Rizvi has been Ahmer Rizvi’s long time mentor. His enormous experience extends over myriad sectors. Being a veteran Management Expert, Professor and author, Dr. Rizvi brings in his A game to transform Reman into a pharma marketing company of international stature. His stint at the esteemed IIT Dhanbad as an acclaimed academician spans for over 15 years. Reman largely relies on Dr.Rizvi’s expertise in matters of corporate communication, negotiation, leadership and managerial effectiveness.

A Ph.D. from Patna University, Dr. Rizvi has served various positions before assuming office as the Advisor at Reman:

  • Assistant Professor, Sultan Qaboos University, Muscat
  • Chariman, IIM, Indore
  • Director, Jaipuria School of Business, New Delhi
  • Director, Jaipuria Institute of Management, New Delhi

Being a veteran corporate trainer and executive coach who has conducted innovative management and leadership development programmes for both public and private sector companies, Dr. Rizvi founded Maxel Business School, an independent business school that strives to make quality management education accessible to marginalized sections of Indian society through innovative management programmes. He is currently the Director & Chairman of Maxel. His consultation services have been sought by several organizations including Ministry of Home, Government of India; Ministry of Health, Government of Madhya Pradesh; Directorate of Industries, Government of Uttar Pradesh; IPCA Pharmaceuticals; Kotak Life Insurance and ONGC. Dr. Rizvi has authored 11 books including a national best seller.

Where We Work

Chandigarh, India

Reman has its roots in Chandigarh, the capital city of two Indian states – Punjab and Haryana. Situated near the foothills of the Shivalik mountain range of the Himalayas, Chandigarh is famous for its well-planned and meticulous infrastructure and architecture. The reliability and quality of the service we provide has helped us bag right from our modest origins, the repute of being one of the best ethical pharma companies based out of the city of Chandigarh. Our workforce and production are both expanding. In the light of the cognizance that our accruing client pool’s requirements call for additional facilities, we are all set to build a new manufacturing factory in Chandigarh, the first planned city of India. Optimum production capacity in times of need is what we intend to accomplish with the new facility. The global pandemic Covid 19 has made the pharmaceutical sector reassess in a new light, the magnitude of medicine dosage necessity worldwide. Our new facility will be all equipped to join the pharmaceutical sector over the globe, in meeting the growing medical requirements of the world.
